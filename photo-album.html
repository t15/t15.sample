How to use
  • Add photos Click "写真を追加" at the bottom, or drag & drop images anywhere.
  • Browse Photos float as bubbles. Drag or swipe to stir them.
  • View Click or tap any bubble to open the photo full-size.
  • Navigate Inside the viewer: swipe left/right, press ← →, or use prev/next. Swipe down to close.
  • Slideshow Click "▶ slideshow" in the viewer to auto-advance photos.
  • Fullscreen Use "⛶ fullscreen" (top right) for immersive viewing.
